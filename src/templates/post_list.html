Oh hello! I’m Emily, a foodie, amateur graphic designer, and soon to be bouldering superstar. You found this blog because (1) I handed you my business card or (2) I applied to your company. Regardless, thank you for stopping by and taking a looking!

There is a plethora of web publishing platforms to choose from, such as the beloved WordPress or Squarespace. All of them offering easy to use templates, enabling the user to hit the ground running. Opting out of those assessable platforms, with a “do it yourself” attitude, this blog was hand crafted from scratch.

My boyfriend Howie and I, did not want “just a blog”, but a project we could collaborate on. We wanted to step into uncharted territory and try something new.

Gimme That was created to showcase his web development skills, my creative endeavors for UI/UX design, and the adventures we share.

Additionally, I make amazing food, love exploring outdoors with my dogs, and playing with graphics. Please bear with us as this site continuously evolves.
